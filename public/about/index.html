About Brad

He's a pretty rad dude

Brad Orego is an Experience Designer, Web Developer, Entrepreneur, and Dancer currently spending time between Brooklyn, NY and Madison, WI (not literally!). This is his personal blog, where you’ll find posts on anything from company culture to experience design to homebrewing to dance.

Brad is a graduate of the University of Rochester with a B.S. in Computer Science, a B.A. in Psychology, and minors in Spanish and Dance. He spends his days working with startups and small businesses, participates in entrepreneurial events and hackathons, dances with Kanopy Dance Company, curls at the Madison Curling Club, plays ultimate frisbee, runs, bikes, homebrews as a proud member of MHTG, makes and blogs about beer-batter pancakes, makes pretentiously long lists of his activities, and even plays old video games from time to time.
